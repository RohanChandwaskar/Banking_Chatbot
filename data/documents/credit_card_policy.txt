Headway Bank — Credit Card Terms & Policy

Platinum Rewards Card
- Annual fee: ₹2,500 (waived if spends exceed ₹3,00,000 in the previous year)
- Credit limit: ₹50,000 to ₹10,00,000 based on income
- Reward rate: 2 points per ₹100 on dining and travel; 1 point elsewhere
- Lounge access: 4 domestic lounge visits per quarter

Interest and charges
- Retail APR: 3.49% per month (41.88% annualized) if the full amount is not paid by the due date
- Cash advance fee: 2.5% of amount (minimum ₹500)
- Late payment fee: ₹1,200 if minimum due is not paid by due date
- Over-limit fee: ₹600 per instance

Billing cycle
Statements are generated on the 5th of each month. Payment due date is 25 days from statement date. Minimum amount due is 5% of total outstanding or ₹500, whichever is higher.

EMI on card
Eligible transactions above ₹5,000 can be converted to EMI at 14–16% per annum for tenures of 3, 6, 9, or 12 months.

Dispute resolution
Report unauthorized transactions within 7 days via app or helpline 1800-123-4567. Provisional credit may be issued within 10 working days pending investigation.

Cancellation
Card can be cancelled anytime with no fee if annual fee was not charged in the current year. Outstanding balance must be cleared before closure.
