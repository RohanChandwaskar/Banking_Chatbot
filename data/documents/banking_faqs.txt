Headway Bank — General Banking FAQs

Savings account
Minimum average balance (MAB) for standard savings: ₹10,000 in metro branches, ₹5,000 in semi-urban. Zero-balance salary accounts available for corporate tie-ups.

Fixed deposits
- Tenure: 7 days to 10 years
- Senior citizen extra rate: +0.50% per annum
- Current FD rates (indicative): 7 days–90 days at 4.5%, 1 year at 7.25%, 3 years at 7.50%
- Premature withdrawal allowed with 1% penalty on applicable rate

NEFT and RTGS
NEFT available 24x7 in batches; RTGS for transfers above ₹2,00,000 during RBI hours. IMPS limit ₹5,00,000 per day for retail customers.

Debit card
Free replacement once per year for damage; ₹200 fee for additional replacements. Daily ATM withdrawal limit ₹50,000 (customizable down to ₹10,000).

KYC update
KYC can be updated via video KYC in the app or by visiting a branch with original PAN and Aadhaar. Update required every 10 years or on address change.

Cheque book
First 25-leaf cheque book free per year; ₹3 per leaf thereafter. Stop payment charge ₹100 per cheque.
