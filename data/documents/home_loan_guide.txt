Headway Bank — Home Loan Guide

Product overview
Home loans for purchase, construction, renovation, and balance transfer. Loan up to 90% of property value for amounts under ₹30 lakh; 80% for higher amounts.

Interest rates
- Floating rate from 8.65% per annum (repo-linked)
- Fixed rate option available for first 3 years at 9.10%
- Women co-applicants receive 0.05% concession

Tenure
Maximum tenure 30 years or until applicant turns 70, whichever is earlier.

Processing
- Processing fee: 0.50% of sanctioned amount (max ₹25,000)
- Legal and technical verification charges borne by customer (~₹8,000–₹15,000)
- Sanction letter valid for 6 months

Tax benefits
Customers may claim deduction under Section 24(b) for interest and 80C for principal as per prevailing IT rules. Bank provides provisional interest certificate each financial year.

Insurance
Property insurance mandatory for the loan tenure. Optional life cover available through partner insurers.

Balance transfer
Transfer existing home loan from another bank; top-up up to 20% of property value available for eligible customers.
