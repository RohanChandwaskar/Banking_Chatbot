Headway Bank — Personal Loans FAQ

What is a personal loan?
A personal loan is an unsecured loan you can use for medical expenses, travel, weddings, or debt consolidation. Loan amounts range from ₹50,000 to ₹25,00,000 depending on your income and credit profile.

What is the interest rate?
Personal loan interest rates start at 10.49% per annum (reducing balance) for salaried customers with a CIBIL score above 750. Rates can go up to 18% for lower credit scores.

What is the tenure?
Repayment tenure is between 12 and 60 months. EMI is calculated on a reducing balance basis.

Eligibility
- Age: 21 to 58 years
- Minimum net monthly income: ₹25,000
- At least 1 year of employment with current employer
- Indian resident with valid KYC

Documents required
- PAN and Aadhaar
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Employment letter or offer letter

Processing fee
A one-time processing fee of 2% of the loan amount (minimum ₹999, maximum ₹10,000) is charged at disbursement.

Prepayment
You may prepay after 12 EMIs. A prepayment charge of 4% applies on the outstanding principal if closed within 24 months; no charge after 24 months.

How to apply
Apply via Headway mobile app, internet banking, or visit any branch. Approval typically takes 24–48 hours for complete applications.
